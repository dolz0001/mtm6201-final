About

Welcome from the world seen via my eyes! I'm Dolzen, a 21-year-old photographer driven to capture the essence of a moment and make it come to life via the lens.

I attempt to capture the spirit of each moment, finding beauty in the everyday, whether it be in the calm landscapes or the busy streets. My passion for photography is more than simply a pastime; it's how I express my creativity and give the world a glimpse into my distinct viewpoint.

You may browse a selection of my artwork—a visual tour via my eyes—on this website. I warmly encourage you to peruse my portfolio's wide array of topics and aesthetics. Every picture I take is proof of my devotion to my work and to showcasing the beauty that is all around us.
